Career Vision

Since she was young, she has been passionate about agriculture in Thailand. She aims to apply her knowledge of technology to build tools that help farmers manage their farms more conveniently and affordably, thereby reducing the cost of agricultural products. This reduction in costs benefits others by making food more affordable and reducing chemical usage to improve health. Her career objective is to become a data scientist and software developer in the agriculture field. Her goal is to lower costs for farmers, enabling them to produce more and better quality products while allowing consumers to enjoy healthy and affordable food. Moreover, she aspires to become a farmer herself and establish a farmers' cooperative in Thailand to ensure sustainability and reduce the impact of middlemen who profit from both farmers and consumers, promoting fair trade within society. This is the reason why she chose to study computer science, with a concentration in Data, GIS, and software development.
